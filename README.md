# Adidas-Sales-Performance-POWER-BI-
## Project Overview
This project presents an interactive **Sales Performance Dashboard** built using **Power BI** to analyze Adidas sales data for the years **2020 and 2021**.
The dashboard provides insights into **regional performance, sales trends, retailer contribution, product categories, and sales channels**, helping stakeholders make data-driven decisions.

## Problem Statement
Adidas operates across multiple regions, retailers, and product categories, generating large volumes of sales data. However, without proper visualization, it becomes difficult to:

* Identify top-performing and underperforming regions
* Track sales and profit trends over time
* Analyze retailer-wise contribution to total sales
* Understand customer preferences across product categories
* Evaluate the effectiveness of different sales channels

**Objective:**
To design an interactive dashboard that transforms raw sales data into actionable insights for improving business performance and strategic decision-making.

## Dashboard Features

### Key KPIs
* Total Sales
* Total Profit
* Units Sold
* Average Selling Price
* Sales per Retailer
### Regional Analysis
* West region identified as the **top-performing region (~270M sales)**
* Midwest recorded the **lowest sales (~136M)**
* Clear regional imbalance highlighting growth opportunities

### Time-Based Analysis
* Monthly and quarterly trends for **Sales & Profit**
* Peak performance observed in **Q3 (July–September)**
* Seasonal variations captured effectively

### Retailer Performance
* **West Gear** is the highest contributing retailer
* **Foot Locker** and **Sports Direct** also major contributors
* Lower contribution from **Amazon and Walmart**

### Product Category Insights
* **Street Footwear** is the top-selling category
* Followed by **Apparel** and **Athletic Footwear**

### Sales Channel Analysis
* **In-store sales (~40%) dominate** overall revenue
* Online sales contribute the least (~27%)
* Indicates opportunity for digital growth

###  Customer Segmentation
* Sales distribution between **Men and Women** categories
* Nearly balanced contribution with slight variation

## Tools & Technologies Used
* **Power BI** – Dashboard creation & visualization
* **Power Query** – Data cleaning and transformation
* **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – Calculated measures and KPIs
* **Microsoft Excel / CSV** – Data source

## Dataset Information
* Source: **Kaggle**
* Data covers:
  * Years: **2020 & 2021**
  * Fields: Region, Retailer, Product Category, Sales, Profit, Units Sold, Sales Method, Date

## Key Insights
* Strong dependency on a few key retailers (West Gear, Foot Locker)
* Significant regional imbalance in sales distribution
* Q3 is the most profitable quarter
* In-store sales dominate, but online channel has growth potential
* Street Footwear drives the majority of revenue
